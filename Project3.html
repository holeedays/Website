Experimental Clock: Assignment #3

Dimensions: 600 px by 600 px Canvas Size

Experimental clocks was my third p5js project. Like the assignment name suggests, I had to make a clock that could tell time in an unconventional sense: hence the keyword "experimental". My clock told time more conventionally but it had a twist to it: The top clock tells the actual world time and the bottom clock tells the procastination time. Every 10 minutes, a pop up asking if you'd like to procastinate for 10 mins or not, will pop up. Everytime you click "procrastinate", the procastinationation clock will start running. It's a weird juxtaposition of a clock telling you how much time you wasted procastinating in relation to the actual time.
