Exquisite Corpse: Assignment #4

"

Dimensions: 400 px by 600 px Canvas Size

Calling back to the first assignment. My fourth assignment, exquisite corpse involved using only 2d primitives
with the incorporation of functions into the mix. The prompt was to share drawings of a charcter with 3 other
people. Then, in the spirit of exquisite corpse, we would take one section of each other's character to
make our exquisite corpse. I ended up getting the head of a king, the body of an alien, and the legs
of an anthropomorphic dog.
