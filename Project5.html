Optical Illusion: Assignment #5

Dimensions: 600 px by 600 px Canvas Size

The fifth assignment involved making any sort of animated optical illusion. I opted to make a simpler optical illusion, the ebbinghaus illusion, but with a more interactive base. Essentially, I made a game out of the illusion. There are two circles surrounded by a ring of other circles. Your goal is to discern whether the two circles are actually the same size or they are slightly different. Just saying, the game is really difficult so I wish you luck if you want to try it out!
