Dimensions: [Original] 600 px by 400 px Canvas Size; Mine is 600 by 1000 px

The final assignment and 7th assignment, autobiographical game, is a culmination of all our projects. Unlike the other assignments, this assignment's prompt was completely open ended: it just had to involve some sort of autobiographical element (it could be very cursory, like in my game). My autobiographical game, titled with the very original name 'Shooter', is a 2d shooter reminiscent of antiquated games like Space Invaders and Asteroids. It has 3 levels, fully functional title and reset buttons, an end/title screen, SFX, and UI elements. To play, use WASD, arrow keys to move and RMB (mouse) to shoot. Enjoy!
